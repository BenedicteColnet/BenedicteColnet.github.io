Bénédicte Colnet

Hi, Welcome to my webpage!

I am Bénédicte Colnet, a third-year Ph.D. candidate under the supervision of Julie Josse, Erwan Scornet, and Gaël Varoquaux at Inria (France).

My current research work is related to applied math and causal inference.

I have a focus on health-related topics. For example I am collaborating with the Traumabase, a French medical registry focused on traumatic injured patients.

I am an engineer by training. I gratuated from École polytechnique in France, and studied bioinformatics in Sorbonne Université. I also am a Corps des Mines civil servant engineer (i.e. working for French administration). I worked in different places and companies during almost three years in total before starting a Ph.D. in September 2020.
