Ph.D. student in statistics & causal inference

Hi! Welcome on my homepage!

I am Bénédicte Colnet, and currently am a Ph.D. candidate under the supervision of Julie Josse, Erwan Scornet, and Gaël Varoquaux at Inria Paris-Saclay.

Research work

My current research work is related to statistics and causal inference. More precisely I try to understand how to get the most of both experimental and observational data. I have a specific focus on health-related topics. For example I am collaborating with the Traumabase, a French medical registry focused on traumatic injured patients.

On this webpage you can find my publications and teaching materials. Don’t hesitate to contact me if you have any questions. I will be more than happy to answer!

Short biography

After high school I hesitated between science and economy, and finally decided to pursue scientific studies. I graduated from École polytechnique and obtained a M.Sc. in bioinformatics from Sorbonne Université. I worked in different places and companies during almost three years in total.
